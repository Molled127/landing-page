Somaliland

Somaliland is an autonomous region in northern Somalia, which broke away and declared independence from Somalia in 1991. No foreign power recognises Somaliland's sovereignty, but it is self-governing with an independent government, its own constitutions, militry and money as well as made democratic elections.
